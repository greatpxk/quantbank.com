GlobalQuant Technology Co., Ltd. is a leading Internet finance company specializing in managing quant investment. As a global finance platform, GlobalQuant aims to provide professional product rating, consulting, analysis and sale service in the quantitative field. QuantBank, a core brand of the company, is designed to target at the middle-income clients.It focuses on giving premium financial service, developing strategies centered on quantitative investment and implementing comprehensive risk control and management. The GlobalQuant team will keep bringing innovative financial experience for every individual investor around the world.

HE Lifeng

CEO

He Lifeng, graduated from Tsinghua University with a B.S. and M.S. in Electronic Engineering, is the founder and CEO of GlobalQuant Technology Co., Ltd. since August 2015, a leading Internet finance company specializing in quant invest and trading. Besides, Mr. He is the author of 3 monographs and more than 10 papers and owns 7 granted patents.
